Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I create a new task?
    A: Tap the '+' button on the main screen and enter your task details.
  • Q: Why do my tasks disappear each day?
    A: HatchTasks gives you a fresh start each day, helping you focus on present tasks without past burdens.
  • Q: How does the egg hatching feature work?
    A: As you complete tasks, you'll see an egg slowly crack and hatch, visually representing your progress.

Troubleshooting

  • App not loading? Try closing and reopening the app. If issues persist, restart your device.
  • Tasks not saving? Ensure you have a stable internet connection and you're using the latest app version.
  • Display issues? Check if your iOS is up to date. HatchTasks works best on the latest iOS version.
